## Breakdown by Source

### PR Newswire (11 acquisitions)

The following private companies announced acquisitions through PR Newswire on December 9, 2025. These include private equity firms, family offices, and privately-held operating companies across various sectors including technology, consulting, manufacturing, and real estate.

**Weinberg Capital Group**, a Cleveland-based private investment firm and family office, acquired **Pinson Valley Heat Treating**, a specialty heat-treating company serving the aerospace industry. The acquisition demonstrates continued consolidation in the industrial services sector.

**Creative Planning**, one of the largest independent registered investment advisors in the United States, acquired **Marshall Financial Group**, an established RIA based in Doylestown, Pennsylvania. The transaction adds more than $900 million in assets under management and 13 employees to Creative Planning's platform.

**Thoma Bravo**, a leading software-focused private equity firm, completed two significant acquisitions. First, the firm completed its previously announced acquisition of **Verint Systems**, a leader in AI-driven customer experience automation, which closed on November 26, 2025. Second, Thoma Bravo completed the acquisition of **PROS Holdings**, a provider of AI-powered SaaS pricing and selling solutions.

**Duco Experts**, an online marketplace connecting expertise in policy and security domains, acquired **Nurdle AI** and appointed co-founder Jeremy Micley as CEO. The acquisition pairs Nurdle's brand and intellectual property with Duco's global expert network to expand capabilities for safe, secure, and responsible AI deployment at enterprise scale.

**Trinity Consultants**, an environmental consulting firm, acquired **Meridian Consultants LLC**. The acquisition enhances Trinity's market-leading environmental compliance and permitting capabilities, expanding CEQA/NEPA and land use expertise across California.

**Cadent**, a portfolio company of private equity firm Novacap, acquired **VuePlanner**, a leader in YouTube video ad planning, optimization, and measurement. The acquisition expands Cadent's capabilities in the converged TV advertising market.

**Sysdyne**, a provider of ready-mix concrete software solutions, acquired **Slabstack**, a pioneer in intelligent pricing, CRM, and AI-driven commercial insights for the heavy building materials industry. The acquisition creates a unified, end-to-end, cloud-native ecosystem for the construction materials sector.

**Monkey in the Metal**, a Baltimore-based architectural millwork shop, completed the acquisition of **KSI Professional**, a 30-year leader in presentation furniture and professional speaker systems. The transaction, finalized on October 16, 2025, creates the Mid-Atlantic's only full-service architectural millwork and presentation systems provider.

**EQT Real Estate**, the real estate investment arm of global investment organization EQT, acquired **Scranton North Logistics Center**, a single-asset acquisition of a 1 million square foot Class A logistics facility strategically located in Northeast Pennsylvania's industrial corridor.

**Janssen Auto Group**, a family-owned dealership group established over 50 years ago, acquired **Gregg Young Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram** of Plattsmouth, Nebraska. The transaction was advised by Performance Brokerage Services and expands Janssen's footprint to eight dealerships.

### Business Wire (8 acquisitions)

The following private companies announced acquisitions through Business Wire on December 9, 2025. These transactions span multiple sectors including electrical services, event management, medical devices, wealth management, real estate, and professional services.

**Bear Castle Capital**, a leading building industry-focused private investment firm, launched a new electrical services platform concurrent with its acquisition of **Colstrip Electrical, Inc.** Founded in 1985, CEI is one of the nation's leading full-service electrical service platforms, providing engineering, design, installation, repair, and monitoring services for complex electrical systems.

**ITA Group**, a global leader in engagement solutions based in West Des Moines, Iowa, announced the strategic acquisition of two award-winning European event agencies: **C2events**, based in West Yorkshire, England, and **Daymakers**, based in Utrecht, Netherlands. Effective October 31, 2025, these acquisitions significantly expand ITA Group's local presence and event capabilities throughout Europe.

**Montagu** and **Kohlberg**, two leading middle-market private equity firms, jointly entered into a definitive agreement to acquire **Teleflex Medical OEM** from Teleflex Incorporated (NYSE: TFX) for $1.5 billion in a carve-out transaction. For over 40 years, Teleflex Medical OEM has developed and supplied custom-engineered interventional catheter components and sub-assemblies, as well as surgical fibers and sutures.

**Earned**, a leading tech-enabled wealth firm purpose-built for healthcare professionals, announced the acquisition of **OJM Group**, an Ohio-based Registered Investment Adviser, financial planning, and insurance agency serving physicians and high-net-worth individuals across the United States for over 25 years. The deal adds nearly $1 billion in assets under management.

**Forum Investment Group**, a private real estate investment management firm based in Denver, announced the acquisition of **The Indigo**, a newly delivered Class A, 168-unit multifamily community located in Canton, Georgia, north of Atlanta. The transaction marks Forum's first real estate acquisition in nearly five years.

**Halstatt Real Estate Partners**, a women-owned real estate private equity firm based in Naples, Florida, announced the acquisition of **Isles at East Millenia**, a 200-unit multifamily community located in Orlando, Florida. The property was acquired in partnership with GoldOller Real Estate Investments.

**Prosperity Partners**, a Chicago-headquartered tax and accounting business backed by private equity firm Unity Partners LP, completed the addition of New York-based **Farkouh, Furman & Faccio**, a provider of tax, attest, accounting, and business consulting services. The company's partners are reinvesting in Prosperity as part of the transaction.
