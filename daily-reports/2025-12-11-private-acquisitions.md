## Acquisition Details

### Fidelity Building Services Group acquires St. Johns Air

Fidelity Building Services Group, a leading provider of HVAC/Mechanical Services, Building Automation, Emergency Power, and Energy Solutions, announced its expansion into the Northeast Florida and South Georgia markets with the acquisition of St. Johns Air. Fidelity is a private company backed by Onex Partners.

### Amca acquires Aerospace Control Products, Inc.

Amca, a Los Angeles-based aerospace and defense company, acquired Aerospace Control Products, Inc. (ACPI) of Davenport, Iowa, expanding its focus on hydraulic components. This acquisition follows Amca's recent acquisition of Cal-Draulics and supports its growing engineering capabilities.

### DMI Solutions acquires Leading Edge Innovations technology

DMI Solutions, a leading provider of beauty and personal care contract manufacturing services, acquired the proprietary process and ingredient technology of Leading Edge Innovations. The acquisition includes intellectual property, equipment, and formulations. DMI Solutions is backed by TruArc Partners.

### OnDeck Partners acquires Montgomery Biscuits and Visalia Rawhide

OnDeck Partners, a newly established sports ownership group within the Avenue Sports Fund led by Marc Lasry, announced its entry into Minor League Baseball with the acquisition of two franchises: the Montgomery Biscuits (Double-A affiliate of the Tampa Bay Rays) and the Visalia Rawhide.

### Reliable Energy acquires Economy Propane

Reliable Energy, backed by Soundcore Capital Partners, announced its acquisition of Economy Propane, LLC. Economy, headquartered in Baltimore, Maryland, is a leading bulk-propane distributor. This acquisition expands Reliable Energy's presence into the Mid-Atlantic region.

### Avocet Partners acquires EMC National Life Insurance Company

Avocet Partners, a newly launched insurance-focused investment and operating platform backed by $500 million of committed equity capital from Oaktree Capital Management and Lane42 Investment Partners, entered into a definitive agreement to acquire EMC National Life Insurance Company from EMC Insurance. The acquisition provides Avocet with a nationally licensed platform operating in 48 states.

### Anything acquires anything.com domain

Anything, an AI platform that lets anyone ship production-ready mobile and web apps, announced its acquisition of the "anything.com" domain for $2 million. The acquisition supports Anything's vision that software is no longer the domain of developers alone.

### Teal acquires Ramped Careers

Teal, the job search and career development platform trusted by 4 million professionals, announced it has acquired Ramped, the AI-powered "Autopilot" platform that automates job applications and accelerates hiring outcomes. The acquisition deepens Teal's investment in building a comprehensive, end-to-end job search platform.

### Veeam completes acquisition of Securiti AI

Veeam completed its $1.725 billion acquisition of Securiti AI, the recognized leader in DSPM, privacy, governance and AI trust. The acquisition creates the industry's first Trusted Data Platform for Accelerating Safe AI at Scale. Veeam is a private company backed by private equity.

### Bluespring Wealth Partners acquires nine firms in 2025

Bluespring Wealth Partners, a leading acquirer of independent RIAs and hybrid wealth management firms, announced the completion of nine acquisitions in 2025, representing more than $6 billion in assets under management. Bluespring is Kestra's fee-only RIA channel.

### Nexo acquires Buenbit

Nexo, the premier digital asset platform with $11 billion in assets under management, announced its acquisition of Buenbit, one of Latin America's most trusted and fastest-growing crypto platforms. The strategic transaction marks a milestone in Nexo's global scaling and expansion across Latin America.

### Joy Parenting Club acquires Heba Care

Joy Parenting Club, the platform revolutionizing the parenting experience, announced its acquisition of Heba Care, a technology platform that supports families navigating complex health and developmental needs. The acquisition marks Joy's latest milestone in building the most comprehensive support ecosystem for parents.
